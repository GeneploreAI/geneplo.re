Bot for events and giveaways

A Giveaway Bot in Discord is a powerful and user-friendly bot designed to facilitate and manage giveaways within a Discord server. With its robust features and customization options, it simplifies the process of organizing and conducting giveaways, ensuring fairness and transparency. This bot allows server administrators to effortlessly set up giveaways by specifying crucial details like the prize, duration, entry requirements, and the number of winners.
The Giveaway Bot also automates notifications and reminders related to the giveaway

You can read more information about GiveawayBot in their site - giveawaybot.party!

Discord bots and servers finder - Top.gg

Top.gg is a popular Discord bot that offers an extensive range of features and utilities to enhance your Discord server. With numerous commands and customizable settings, this bot aims to make managing and moderating your community a breeze.
One of the key features of top.gg is its advanced moderation tools. It provides auto-moderation features such as anti-spam filters, word filters, and anti-raid protection.
